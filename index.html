Our Mission

The Goal of Charter Residential

We exist to help landlords earn more from the exact same property. Traditional property management takes a fixed cut whether your property performs well or not. We operate on a Performance-Based Model—meaning our success is mathematically tied to your success.

100% Aligned Incentives

We earn our premium strictly on the rent recovered above your agreed base. If you don't profit, we don't profit.

You Keep Control

You maintain full ownership and final say over structural changes. You hold the asset, we operate the system.

Reclaim Your Time

We handle multi-channel marketing, 24/7 tenant communications, and minor repairs. Your real estate truly becomes passive.

Dedicated Support

We act as the single point of contact, providing seamless customer service for both tenants and landlords to ensure a frictionless experience.

Clear Division of Responsibility

Included (Charter Covers)

  • Multi-channel property marketing
  • Professional tenant screening & placement
  • Seamless lease onboarding
  • Automated rent tracking & collection
  • 24/7 direct tenant communication
  • Minor repairs (plumbing/electrical up to $150/mo)

Owner Responsibility

  • Utilities payment
  • Major repairs (above $150 threshold)
  • Structural work & municipal permits
  • Property taxes
  • Mortgage payments
  • Homeowner's insurance

About Us

Redefining Modern Construction in the GTA.

Charter Residential is a top builder of modern garden and modular suites in the Greater Toronto Area (GTA). We handle the entire process for you—from the first design and city permits all the way to the final build. Our goal is to help you get the most out of your property, whether you want more living space for your family or a new way to earn passive rental income.

We are excited to share our new line of eco-friendly, net-zero modular homes. They are carefully designed to fit perfectly in backyards of all shapes and sizes. We are dedicated to building sustainable, beautiful, and highly useful spaces that add real, long-term value to your home.
